About Me

Hi there! I hope you're having a good day so far.

I'm Aaron Soliz, an aspiring software engineer and front-end developer based in Sacramento, California. My journey into computer science began when I joined a club in the fourth grade and created a cute website with my friends about the ancient wonders of the world. Whenever I have my hands on a keyboard (or a pencil when I feel like sketching), the feeling of creating something new from the tips of my fingers and watching the end result slowly form feels satisfying. Even during all those nights where I get caught up in what I'm doing and realize I'm up later than I should be.

Fast forward to now, I'm proud to say that I am a graduate from the California State University of Sacramento and earned a Bachelor's degree in Computer Engineering in 2023. When I'm not watching the latest blockbusters or renowned classics, I'm expanding my working knowledge and have found my calling in software and front-end development. You can check out some projects I've worked on here.

Being on the autism spectrum, struggles in academics and social skills presented themselves. But with help from my family, friends, and the community that I was raised in, I was able to give it everything I got and perform to the best of my ability in my academics. Every day, I am grateful that I had a good team that supported me in every step in my journey, and this eventually became the foundation of my work philosophy. I believe that as innovators, we are stronger as a team, and I would love to be a part of yours.

What I can bring to the table, in addition to my set of skills, is a smile on my face and a good attitude. I can be a focused teammate that will always have reaching goals and the bigger picture in mind. If there are things that I'm not familiar with, I can make up for it by being a quick and eager learner so that I can get up to speed and be an effective contributor. Most importantly, I will be a member that wants to engage in insightful discussions with teammates, whether I'm presenting ideas or listening to yours.
